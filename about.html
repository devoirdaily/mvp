What does "Devoir" mean?

Our North Star

Devoir is a mission-driven startup committed to providing innovative solutions to support health, dignity, and convenience in underserved communities such as Camden.

Our name reflects our commitment to ensure everyone has access to nutritious, convenient meals that fuel their potential and strengthen their communities.
