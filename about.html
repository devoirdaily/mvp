What does "Devoir" mean?

Our North Star

Devoir started with a simple belief: Every meal is a chance to build civility, dignity, and opportunity in overlooked places.

From Food Desert to Food Oasis

Camden has been classified as a food desert for too long. Corner stores with processed snacks and fast food shouldn't be the only convenient options when you're rushing to work or need to feed your family.

We're changing that narrative, one nutrient-packed parfait at a time. Our name "Devoir" means "duty" in French—because we believe it's our duty to ensure everyone has access to meals that fuel their potential and strengthen their communities.
